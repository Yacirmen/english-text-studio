About ReadLex What the product is designed to help with.

ReadLex is a focused reading studio for learners who want more than quick translation. It turns a reading session into a loop: choose a text, tap useful words or phrases, save them, and come back through quiz and review.

  • Built around short reading sessions instead of heavy lesson screens.
  • Designed to keep meaning, context, and example usage tied together.
  • Made for gradual retention rather than one-time lookup.

Privacy What user data this version stores and why.

ReadLex stores only the information needed to support account access and review features in this version.

  • Username and password hash are stored to support account access.
  • Saved words, review results, and reading history are stored to power recall features.
  • Session cookies are used to keep signed-in users logged in securely.
  • This version should not be treated as a place to enter sensitive personal information.
Terms of Use The expected boundaries for this current release.
  • ReadLex is offered as a learning tool and may continue to change as the product evolves.
  • Users should not rely on a single word card as professional, legal, or medical advice.
  • Service quality, reading availability, and account features may be refined between releases.
  • Abuse, scraping, or attempts to disrupt the experience may lead to access being removed.
